# B-B-C Lab @ UT Austin
#### PI: Dr. Xue-Xin Wei
We use theoretical, computational and statistical approaches to study the neural basis of behavior and cognition. We work with various kinds of data, including data from behavioral, electrophysiological and calcium imaging experiments.
